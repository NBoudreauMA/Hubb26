Understanding Hubbardston's Revenue Sources

The Town of Hubbardston funds its operations through a mix of revenue sources. This page breaks down where our money comes from and how it supports the community.

Primary Revenue Sources

  • Property Taxes: The largest funding source, providing essential services like schools, public safety, and infrastructure.
  • State Aid: Funds allocated by Massachusetts to supplement local budgets.
  • Local Receipts: Fees, permits, excise taxes, and other town-generated revenues.
  • Grants & Other Funds: Special funding opportunities for targeted projects.

How Revenue Supports the Budget

Revenue directly influences town services, infrastructure investments, and long-term financial planning. Understanding how our funds are generated helps shape smarter spending decisions.
