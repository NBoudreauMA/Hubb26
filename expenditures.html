Fiscal Year 2026 Expenditures

The Fiscal Year 2026 budget reflects the Town of Hubbardston's commitment to maintaining essential services while strategically allocating resources for future growth and efficiency. This year's expenditures address personnel adjustments, infrastructure investments, and operational efficiencies, balancing fiscal responsibility with the town's evolving needs.

Key Budgetary Decisions

Personnel Adjustments

  • Increase in Executive Assistant salary (+11%) to better align with responsibilities and market rates.
  • Town Administrator salary adjusted for a cost-of-living increase (+3%).
  • Expanded custodial services (+63%) to support facility maintenance across town buildings.
  • Select Board budget modifications include reallocations for postage (+300%) and advertising (-67%).

Technology & Security Investments

  • IT maintenance funding increased (+9%) to support cybersecurity enhancements and modernized infrastructure.
  • Website funding maintained to ensure continued resident engagement and digital accessibility.

Education & Public Safety

  • Quabbin Regional School District assessment increased (+8%) due to rising enrollment and operating costs.
  • Dispatch services funding increased (+6%) to maintain 911 and emergency response capabilities.
  • Investment in police equipment (+176%) to modernize and ensure officer safety.

Infrastructure & Maintenance

  • Road materials budget increased (+10%) to accommodate higher costs and maintenance needs.
  • Expansion of municipal building maintenance funding (+30%) to address deferred maintenance.
  • Elimination of longevity program (-$2,000) as part of personnel compensation restructuring.

Public Services & Human Resources

  • Council on Aging Director hours expanded (+31%) to enhance senior services and outreach.
  • Reduction in veteran benefits (-50%) based on historical trends while maintaining essential support programs.
  • Library staff wage adjustments (+6%) to support extended programming hours.

Conclusion

The FY26 budget prioritizes financial stability while addressing essential service needs and long-term sustainability. By balancing investments in personnel, infrastructure, and technology, Hubbardston is well-positioned to navigate upcoming challenges while fostering responsible fiscal management. The proposed budget reflects careful planning to ensure continued service excellence and prudent financial stewardship.
