Key Insights & Context

Tax Bill Context: Hubbardston's average single-family tax bill has shown modest increases over the fiscal years from $5,128 in FY2022 to $6,212 in FY2025, representing a 21.1% increase over four years. This growth rate is relatively aligned with surrounding communities, maintaining Hubbardston's position as providing competitive tax rates within the region.

Property Values: The town has seen healthy property value appreciation from $321,675 in FY2022 to $394,750 in FY2025, a 22.7% increase. This growth reflects Hubbardston's desirability as a residential community while still maintaining more affordable housing compared to towns like Princeton.

Regional Position: Hubbardston's state rank has improved slightly from 146 in FY2022 to 135 in FY2025, moving the town up in Massachusetts municipality rankings. This balanced position suggests Hubbardston maintains fiscal responsibility while improving service quality.
