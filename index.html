FY26 Budget Summary

Welcome & Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Summary

Welcome to Hubbardston’s Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Portal—a space dedicated to transparency, smart financial planning, and ensuring that every dollar spent aligns with our town’s long-term vision.

This year’s budget reflects our ongoing commitment to stability, strategic investments, and responsible financial management. With a total budget of $11,851,233—a 6.32% increase over FY25—we’re ensuring that critical services, infrastructure, and public safety remain priorities. At the same time, projected revenues of $11,719,755 represent a 5.75% growth, reinforcing Hubbardston’s financial health while maintaining our commitment to prudent spending.

Key investments in infrastructure, public safety, and modernization continue to drive our fiscal strategy. This budget also strengthens our financial position by responsibly using $131,478 in free cash, targeting one-time expenses that improve services while reducing future financial strain.

What’s new? We’re taking a modernized approach to budget engagement. This new web-based portal provides real-time access to financial data, spending priorities, and insights into how Hubbardston is planning for a sustainable future. Our commitment remains clear: ensuring long-term financial health while delivering high-quality services that enhance our community.

Thank you for taking the time to engage with this process—we encourage residents to explore the budget, provide feedback, and be part of shaping Hubbardston’s future.

Respectfully submitted,

Nathan Boudreau
Town Administrator

Key Investments & Priorities

Hubbardston’s Fiscal Year 2026 budget prioritizes strategic investments that enhance services, improve infrastructure, and modernize town operations. Below are the core areas of focus shaping this year’s financial plan.

Public Works & Infrastructure

  • Increased funding for road maintenance and winter operations to ensure safer and more reliable public infrastructure.
  • Investments in essential DPW equipment to enhance long-term efficiency and proactive maintenance strategies.
  • Strategic planning for long-term infrastructure improvements while maintaining fiscal responsibility.

Planning & Land Use

  • Expanded resources for the Town Planner, who has been instrumental in securing grants and streamlining land-use planning.
  • Improved permitting processes to support responsible growth and economic development.
  • Long-term land-use planning to ensure sustainable development and increased financial stability.

Modernizing Town Operations

  • Strengthening administrative capacity with expanded roles to improve public access and constituent responsiveness.
  • Ongoing efforts to align fee schedules with regional standards for fairness, transparency, and sustainability.
  • Investments in technology, including Hubb Connect, e-permitting, and automated workflows, ensuring a modern, efficient, and resident-focused government.

Strategic Use of Free Cash

Hubbardston continues to reduce its reliance on free cash, ensuring that these one-time funds are used strategically for critical investments without creating future budget gaps. This year’s allocation of $123,007 supports key initiatives, including infrastructure improvements, public services, and essential operational needs—all while maintaining fiscal responsibility.

By carefully applying free cash, we strengthen service levels, invest in necessary resources, and avoid costly deferred maintenance, ensuring long-term financial stability.

Key Considerations & Fiscal Oversight

  • Education Costs: The Quabbin assessment remains the town’s largest financial risk. If costs exceed current estimates, mid-year adjustments may be required.
  • Revenue Tracking: Close monitoring of local receipts—such as excise taxes and public safety revenue—will be essential to ensure projections remain on target.
  • Long-Term Sustainability: The town continues to reduce free cash dependency, working toward a balanced budget without relying on one-time funds.
  • Budget Flexibility: While revenues are growing, expenses—particularly in education and municipal obligations—are rising faster. A disciplined financial approach remains essential to maintaining stability.

This year’s budget reinforces Hubbardston’s commitment to responsible financial management, service continuity, and future growth. By maintaining strong oversight and making targeted investments, we are positioning the town for long-term success.
