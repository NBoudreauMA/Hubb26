Key Investments & Priorities

Hubbardston's Fiscal Year 2026 budget prioritizes strategic investments that enhance services, improve infrastructure, and modernize town operations. Below are the core areas of focus shaping this year's financial plan.

Public Works & Infrastructure

  • Increased funding for road maintenance and winter operations to ensure safer and more reliable public infrastructure.
  • Investments in essential DPW equipment to enhance long-term efficiency and proactive maintenance strategies.
  • Strategic planning for long-term infrastructure improvements while maintaining fiscal responsibility.

Planning & Land Use

  • Expanded resources for the Town Planner, who has been instrumental in securing grants and streamlining land-use planning.
  • Improved permitting processes to support responsible growth and economic development.
  • Long-term land-use planning to ensure sustainable development and increased financial stability.

Modernizing Town Operations

  • Strengthening administrative capacity with expanded roles to improve public access and constituent responsiveness.
  • Ongoing efforts to align fee schedules with regional standards for fairness, transparency, and sustainability.
  • Investments in technology, including Hubb Connect, e-permitting, and automated workflows, ensuring a modern, efficient, and resident-focused government.

Public Safety Enhancement

  • Increased funding for police and fire departments with a total budget of $1,584,822 for FY26.
  • Investment in modernized equipment and training to improve emergency response capabilities.
  • Enhanced community safety programs and proactive public safety initiatives.
