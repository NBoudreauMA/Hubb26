Legal Hold & Evidence Hooks

  • Authorization: Town Counsel or the Town Administrator triggers holds through the Records Orchestrator interface; approval captured as a signed decision artifact with retentionPolicy reference.
  • Effect: Legal hold pauses the disposition clock and flags legalHoldFlag=true across SuttonCLERK, SuttonFIX, and PERMIT; audit log reflects initiator, timestamp, and justification.
  • Evidence outputs: ARCHIEVE™ produces immutable evidence packages containing hash manifests, archive receipt number, and archieveEvidenceId referenced in each case history.
  • Release: Hold removal requires dual acknowledgment (Town Counsel + Department Head) and regenerates a confirmation receipt stored with the case narrative.

Export & Portability Playbook

  • Routine exports: Quarterly JSON and CSV snapshots for active modules; annual PDF-A compilations for Select Board briefing binders.
  • On-demand pulls: Secure API endpoints return filtered datasets by retentionPolicy or archieveEvidenceId; authorized custodians include Town Clerk (records), DPW Superintendent (SuttonFIX), and Building Commissioner (PERMIT).
  • Custodian cadence: Custodians perform monthly validation checks with checksum verification; IT archives export logs in VAULT™ evidence ledger.
  • Transfer protocols: Encrypted SFTP or USB with AES-256 hardware encryption for state oversight bodies; all transfers produce export manifests stored in ARCHIEVE™.

Acceptance Criteria — VAULT™ Layer

  • Each recordable action in SuttonCLERK, SuttonFIX, and PERMIT stores a retentionPolicy identifier and archieveEvidenceId when evidence is generated.
  • Legal hold toggles pause disposition clocks and display banner messaging to staff until release.
  • Destruction events automatically issue certificates with policy ID, destroyed timestamp, and approving official.
  • Evidence packages are referenceable via archieveEvidenceId inside case timelines and export manifests.
  • Retention reports exportable in CSV/PDF-A, including policy coverage, hold status, and pending destructions.

E. SOP Workflows (SuttonCLERK, SuttonFIX, PRR)

SuttonCLERK Licensing Workflow

  • Trigger: Resident submits licensing application via portal or counter intake.
  • SLA Clock: Starts at submission; pauses during applicant corrections; target decision [SLA_HOURS=72].
  • Decision Points: Completeness check → Board review → Issuance.
  • Notifications: Confirmation email, Board agenda alert, issuance notice with retention summary.
  • Escalation: If pending >48 hours, Town Administrator receives alert.
  • Evidence Checkpoints: Application packet, vote record, license certificate exported with archieveEvidenceId.
# workflow: suttonclerk_licensing
workflow:
  name: SuttonCLERK Licensing
  trigger: application_submitted
  sla:
    target_hours: 72
    pause_conditions:
      - awaiting_applicant_documents
  steps:
    - id: intake_validation
      actions:
        - assign_to: ClerkOffice
        - tag_retention: VAULT-RULE-LIC-07
    - id: board_review
      actions:
        - notify: SelectBoard
        - record_vote: true
    - id: issuance
      actions:
        - generate_license_id: true
        - capture_evidence: archieveEvidenceId
  escalation:
    threshold_hours: 48
    notify: TownAdministrator
  audit:
    exports:
      - type: pdfa_certificate
        reference: archieveEvidenceId

SuttonFIX Service Request Workflow

  • Trigger: Resident logs an issue (road, tree, stormwater) via portal, hotline, or staff entry.
  • SLA Clock: Begins upon intake; pauses for weather or third-party coordination; target completion [SLA_HOURS=48].
  • Decision Points: Priority assignment → Crew dispatch → Verification.
  • Notifications: Resident status updates, crew assignment texts, completion confirmation with photo evidence.
  • Escalation: DPW Superintendent notified at 36 hours if unresolved.
  • Evidence Checkpoints: Before/after photos, work log, resident acknowledgment tied to archieveEvidenceId.

Public Records Request Workflow

  • Trigger: PRR received via portal, email import, or walk-in entry.
  • SLA Clock: Starts at receipt; statutory response due in 10 calendar days; clock pauses when awaiting requester clarification.
  • Decision Points: Clarification required → Fee assessment → Record retrieval → Release.
  • Notifications: Receipt acknowledgement, clarification request, release notification with download instructions.
  • Escalation: Records Access Officer alerted at day 5; Town Counsel at day 8 if unresolved.
  • Evidence Checkpoints: Intake timestamp, retrieval log, redaction notes, export manifest referencing archieveEvidenceId.
# workflow: public_records_request
workflow:
  name: Public Records Request
  trigger: request_received
  sla:
    statutory_days: 10
    pause_conditions:
      - awaiting_requester_clarification
  steps:
    - id: intake
      actions:
        - assign_to: RecordsAccessOfficer
        - tag_retention: VAULT-RULE-PRR-06
    - id: review
      actions:
        - assess_fee: optional
        - coordinate_departments: true
    - id: release
      actions:
        - perform_redaction: true
        - export_package: archieveEvidenceId
        - notify_requester: true
  escalation:
    checkpoints:
      - day: 5
        notify: RecordsAccessOfficer
      - day: 8
        notify: TownCounsel
  audit:
    exports:
      - type: manifest_pdfa
        include: archieveEvidenceId

G. Security, Privacy, and Retention

  • Role-based access: Least privilege roles for Clerk staff, DPW crews, inspectors, and administrators; periodic access reviews every quarter.
  • Logging: Immutable audit logs capturing authentication, data access, legal hold toggles, and export activity with archieveEvidenceId references.
  • Privacy & redaction: Redaction workflow for PRR; sensitive fields masked before export; audit log records redaction officer.
  • Encryption: TLS 1.2+ for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest; key material managed via municipal hardware security module or equivalent escrow.
  • Retention enforcement: VAULT™ ensures policy binding; ARCHIEVE™ manages immutable evidence snapshots with certificate outputs.
