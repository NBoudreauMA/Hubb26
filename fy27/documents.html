FY27 Budget documents

The Document Center automates the FY27 workflow. Files published here appear across the Budget Hub and keep residents informed. Upload tagged, accessible PDFs with selectable text and bookmarks for each stage.

Document Center structure

Folder Purpose Workflow role
FY27 Budget (root) Public landing folder with quick links surfaced across the hub. Finance/TA publish final files.
Draft Internal drafts, department submissions, preliminary presentations. Finance authors; notifies subscribers when promoted.
Hearing Agenda packets, slide decks, and backup posted before Finance Committee hearings. Finance publishes; Clerk links agendas.
Adopted Final appropriations, motions, and book adopted at Town Meeting. Finance publishes after vote; triggers Notify Me alert.
Archive Automatically populated after FY28 launch. Maintains redirect to /budget/archive/fy27/. Finance manages archive move with Records Retention.

How automation works

Whenever a file is published to the FY27 folder tree, the Document Center RSS feed updates instantly. This page and the overview widget refresh automatically, so you only maintain content in one place.

  • Make sure PDFs are tagged and pass accessibility checks.
  • Use descriptive titles like “FY27 Budget Book – Draft 2”.
  • Set the correct workflow stage (Draft, Hearing, Adopted) before publishing.

Draft → Hearing → Adopted workflow

1

Draft authoring

Finance staff upload new documents to the Draft folder. Notify Me alerts stay off until promoted.

2

Publish for hearing

Move the file to the Hearing folder, attach it to the matching agenda item, and enable email/SMS alerts.

3

Adopt and archive

After Town Meeting, republish to the Adopted folder. When FY28 launches, move all FY27 content to /budget/archive/fy27/.
