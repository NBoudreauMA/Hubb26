FY27 FAQ & contact

Get answers about the FY27 budget timeline, accessible documents, and the Notify Me alert system. Use the contact form to send detailed feedback directly to Finance.

Frequently asked questions

How do I access budget files if I use assistive technology?

All PDFs posted to the FY27 folders are tagged, include bookmarks, and pass accessibility checks for screen readers. If you encounter an issue, email budget@suttonma.gov.

Where do Notify Me alerts come from?

Alerts are triggered when Finance moves a document from Draft to Hearing or Adopted, or when the Agenda Center posts a new event tagged “Budget FY27”.

What happens when FY28 launches?

Content migrates into /budget/archive/fy27/ with redirects so links remain valid. Historical content stays accessible on the Budget Hub.

Which system is the source of truth?

HubbCONNECT retains internal financial records, while this FY27 Budget Hub provides curated public access and dashboards.

Contact the Finance Team

You will receive a response within two business days. Public comments submitted become part of the FY27 budget record.
