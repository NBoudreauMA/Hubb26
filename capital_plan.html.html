Capital Improvement Plan (FY26-30)

Plan Overview

The Capital Improvement Plan (CIP) outlines Hubbardston's strategy for investing in the future. It details essential infrastructure projects and improvements planned for the next five fiscal years. This plan focuses on maintaining, upgrading, and expanding key services in the community.
