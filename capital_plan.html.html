Financial Impact & Benefits

Understanding the Financial Impact

The Capital Improvement Plan represents a balanced approach to addressing Hubbardston's infrastructure needs while maintaining fiscal responsibility. The plan is designed to:

  • Distribute Costs Strategically

    The plan spreads major investments across multiple fiscal years to prevent dramatic tax increases in any single year.

  • Leverage Outside Funding

    Many projects are structured to maximize grant opportunities, reducing the direct cost to Hubbardston taxpayers.

  • Prioritize Critical Infrastructure

    The plan addresses the most urgent needs first, focusing on public safety, accessibility, and infrastructure that affects the most residents.

  • Include Sustainability Measures

    The plan incorporates environmental considerations such as reduced salt usage (brine applicator) and energy-efficient lighting (Main Street project).
