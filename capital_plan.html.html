Capital Improvement Plan (FY26-30)

The Capital Improvement Plan (CIP) outlines Hubbardstonâ€™s strategy for investing in the future. It details essential infrastructure projects and improvements planned for the next five years. This plan focuses on maintaining, upgrading, and expanding key services in the community.

Overview

This five-year plan totals $5.5 million, with $2.8 million allocated for FY26. Projects include road maintenance, public safety equipment, technology upgrades, and more. The plan is designed to be fiscally responsible while addressing the town's long-term needs.
